# 📊 Customer Behavior Analysis (SQL | Python | Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This is an end-to-end data analytics project focused on analyzing customer behavior using real-world datasets.

The project transforms raw data into actionable business insights by combining **Python for data processing**, **SQL for analysis**, and **Power BI for visualization**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior  
- Identify high-value and low-value customers  
- Understand purchase frequency and spending patterns  
- Deliver data-driven business insights  

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- **Python (Pandas)** → Data cleaning & exploratory analysis  
- **SQL** → Data querying & business analysis  
- **Power BI** → Dashboard creation & data visualization  

---

## 🔄 Project Workflow

### 1️⃣ Data Cleaning & Preparation (Python)
- Handled missing values and inconsistent data  
- Removed duplicates to ensure data integrity  
- Converted data types for accurate analysis  
- Engineered features such as:
  - `purchase_frequency_days`  
  - `age_group`  

---

### 2️⃣ Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Performed statistical analysis (mean, median, mode)  
- Analyzed customer spending distribution    
- Discovered key behavioral trends  

---

### 3️⃣ Data Analysis (SQL)
- Wrote queries for customer segmentation  
- Identified top customers by revenue  
- Analyzed purchase frequency patterns  
- Evaluated revenue contribution by category  

---

### 4️⃣ Customer Segmentation
- Applied **RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis**  
- Segmented customers into:
  - High-value customers  
  - Loyal customers  
  - At-risk customers  
  - Low-value customers  

---

### 5️⃣ Visualization (Power BI)
- Built an interactive dashboard with:
  - Revenue KPIs  
  - Customer insights  
  - Trend analysis  
- Created visuals for customer segmentation and behavior  

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- The Clothing category generates the highest revenue (~44.6% of total revenue)
- Young Adults contribute significantly (~26.6% of total revenue), making them a key customer segment
- A large portion of customers are non-subscribers, indicating an opportunity to improve engagement and retention

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus on expanding and promoting Clothing products, as they contribute nearly half of total revenue
- Target Young Adults with personalized marketing campaigns and offers to maximize revenue
- Convert non-subscribers into subscribers through discounts, loyalty programs, or exclusive benefits
- Improve customer retention by encouraging repeat purchases and long-term engagement
